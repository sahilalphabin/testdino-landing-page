Testdino is smart test reporting and analytics platform built for Playwright and Selenium. It helps QA and dev teams analyze test failures faster, detect flakiness, and ship with confidence.

Testdino connects to your CI pipeline (GitHub Actions, GitLab, etc.) and analyzes every test run. It collects execution data, classifies failures using AI, and visualizes trends across branches, commits, and environments .

Testdino’s AI doesn’t just report failures — it explains them.

Powered by a trained language model, it reads your test failures like a senior developer and suggests the fix. Whether it’s a
flaky selector, a UI change, or a real bug, you’ll get a root-cause diagnosis with confidence scores, historical context, and
actionable recommendations.
