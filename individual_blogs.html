Imagine downloading an app, and inputting your personal data, and a hacker in seconds finds a flaw to steal your information. Sounds scary, right? And today, mobile app security is no longer an add-on, it's the basis for user trust and business integrity.

The overall global average cost per data breach was $4.88M and the highest spending emerged from the healthcare industry. Data protection of customers is critical to the stability of mobile apps, so client trust and confidence remain intact.

Your defense should also get smarter, because so should cybercriminals. So when there are so many security providers out there, how do you choose the right one? That is where we come in! Why Mobile App Security Can't Be an Afterthought?

Before choosing a security provider, ensure they tick all the right boxes:

Data Protection

Safeguarding sensitive user information and business data from unauthorized access.

Compliance

Meeting industry standards and regulatory requirements for data security.

Reputation

Protecting brand trust and customer confidence through secure practices.

Business Continuity

Ensuring uninterrupted operations and service delivery to customers.

Competitive Advantage

Gaining market differentiation through superior security measures.

What Makes a Mobile App Secure?

A secure mobile app should implement multiple layers of protection to safeguard user data and maintain trust.

Encryption

End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest.

Secure Authentication

Robust login systems with biometric and multi-factor options.

Regular Security Updates

Continuous patching and vulnerability management.

24/7 Support

Security Practices and Protocols

Implementing robust security practices is essential for protecting mobile applications from evolving threats.

Secure Development Lifecycle

Integrating security into every phase of app development.

Penetration Testing

Incident Response

Prepared protocols for handling security breaches.
